Problem Statement

We are on a mission to improve the efficiency and decision making for commercial contracts in the HVAC industry. Can this bring better economical benefits and ethical standard?


Questions

In order to get a good understanding, we identified the following pain points:


  1. Time consuming negotiations.
  2. Fresh analytics performed for each client or project.
  3. No public repository of business contacts/profiles to reach out.
  4. Hard to keep track of contract progress and people involved.
  5. Every contract supplies different formats of Excel BOM to quote for.
Solutions

Technical Repository

Every OEM and client can find a library of frequently updated technical information to keep track of/compare.


Contracting & Analytics

The client can add and manage new contracts by comparing the Business partner, their ratings and past project execution portfolios. The contracts are stored and the analytics presented for better management decisions.

Results

Conclusions

  1. We have received some great feedback from the markets we tested in.
  2. Certain hypothesis and feature ideas were pivoted from and we were pleasantly surprised by those market inputs.
  3. We tested and survey ex-colleagues, companies and clients and we must surely expand the scope of research.
  4. We are making slow and steady progress, expect a working prototype soon!
